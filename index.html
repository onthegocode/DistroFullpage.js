Who is DistroSwag?

DistroSwag is an NYC based wholesale distributor that strives to provide its patrons with high quality goods at competitively low prices and outstanding client services. We specialize in business to business servicing, and we are adaptable to the specific needs of any business. DistroSwag prides itself on developing close relationships with and delivering superior service to its patrons.

Our Goals

Our goal is to continue to grow as an organization and provide outstanding products and customer service to our clients. We will continue to scale our business to include an expanding variety of quality goods, and we will accommodate as much clientele as possible without sacrificing efficiency or customer satisfaction. Lastly, our goal is to continue to develop long term relationships with the businesses we work with, as we continue to evolve as a company and help others start and grow their businesses.
